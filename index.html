Welcome to Diet Meal Generator

Every person needs an amount of calories to provide energy to get through the day. Sometimes we eat more calories than we actually need, here is when we gain weight, and sometimes we eat approximately how much we need. When we make a diet we create a plan of meals that create what we call a caloric deficit, basically eat fewer calories than we need, which makes us use the glucose and fat reserves from our body as an energy source and lose weight.
